How We Work

A process built on
honest work.

Four phases. Clear deliverables at each stage. No surprises, no scope creep, no revision roulette.

01
Phase One
Discovery & Strategy

We spend two weeks learning your business, audience, and competitive context before touching a single pixel. Interviews, audits, benchmarking, positioning workshops. The most important work happens before the creative work.

1–2 weeks
02
Phase Two
Creative Direction

One creative direction — not three options that dilute focus. We present a fully committed visual and conceptual framework, backed by the rationale from Phase One. You respond, we refine.

1–2 weeks
03
Phase Three
Design & Build

The execution phase. Pixel-perfect Figma files, production code, or both — depending on scope. Weekly check-ins, transparent feedback loops, and a staging environment you can click through before we go live.

3–8 weeks
04
Phase Four
Launch & Handoff

A clean handoff package: source files, documentation, guidelines, and a recorded walkthrough. 30-day post-launch support. And a standing invitation to reach out when the next chapter begins.

1 week + 30d support
