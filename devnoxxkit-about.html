Our Story

We are a
studio built on
obsession.

We believe that anonymous is not safe — it's invisible. That design is not decoration. That the best work earns silence before it earns applause. That every pixel is either earning its place or wasting space. That clients deserve honesty more than agreement. That good enough is the enemy of everything worth making.

The Origin

Started with
one rule: no mediocrity.

DevnoxxKit began in a 14-square-metre studio in Lisbon with two designers, one client, and a shared conviction that the creative industry was producing too much forgettable work at too low a price. We decided to do the opposite.

Thirteen years later, we've worked with over 230 clients across 40 countries. We've been featured on Awwwards, FWA, and Dezeen. We've shipped work that has won D&AD Wood Pencils and Red Dot awards. We still work in the same way we always have: one project at a time, with the full team's attention, from strategy to final pixel.

We don't take every project we're offered. We take the ones we can do justice to — the ones where the client's ambition matches ours.

Our History

Thirteen years of
building things right.

2013
Founded in Lisbon

Two designers, a shared conviction, and a 14-square-metre studio. First client: a Lisbon restaurant that needed more than a logo. We gave them a complete identity and a philosophy.

2016
First international client

A Berlin fintech startup found us through Dribbble. We designed their identity and web presence remotely — before remote work was fashionable. The work won a Red Dot nomination.

2019
Expanded to 8 people

Added our first brand strategist, motion designer, and frontend architect. Deliberately stopped at eight. The work got sharper, not broader.

2022
First D&AD recognition

The Meridian Capital rebrand earned a Wood Pencil — the first of what would become a consistent presence in international award circuits.

2024
DevnoxxKit launched

After three years of internal use, we opened up our design system to the world. It became one of the top-rated HTML templates on Gumroad within 90 days.

2026
Today

230+ projects. 40+ countries. The same eight people. Open for Q3 2026.
